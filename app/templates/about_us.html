Our Services


Contingent Search

Inuka collaborates with you to pinpoint and secure top-tier talent with the expertise necessary to meet your objectives and create a meaningful impact. We specialise in identifying and securing top talent on a flexible basis, ensuring a responsive approach to your workforce requirements. We aim to enhance your team with skilled professionals, offering a dynamic and adaptable staffing solution for your evolving business needs.


Retained Search

We offer exclusive and dedicated services to secure top-tier talent for critical positions at your firm. Our approach involves a deep understanding of your organisational needs, ensuring a thorough and strategic search process. By retaining our services, you gain access to our extensive network and expertise, resulting in a customised recruitment strategy. We prioritise precision and excellence, and are committed to delivering long-term value by identifying and placing candidates who align seamlessly with your company's goals and culture.


Board Services

Inuka is dedicated to discovering individuals who will fortify your board and elevate your senior team. We bring innovative perspectives, complementary skills, and a diverse range of experiences at the executive level. Our commitment lies in identifying candidates who can bring added strength and depth to your leadership, ensuring that your top-tier executives are well equipped to navigate challenges and drive success.


Salary Benchmarking

Our approach to salary benchmarking is rooted in a commitment to providing comprehensive insights that empower your organisation. We deliver accurate and up-to-date salary data to help you make informed decisions in aligning your compensation structures with industry standards. By conducting thorough salary benchmarking analyses, we aim to ensure that your organisation remains competitive in attracting and retaining top talent.
